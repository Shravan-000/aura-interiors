Who we are

Design is not just what it looks like — it's how it feels

Aura Interiors is a Mumbai-based design studio specializing in residential and commercial spaces. We believe every space has a soul, and our job is to bring it to life.

From initial concept to final execution, we handle every detail — spatial planning, 3D visualization, material selection, and project management.
